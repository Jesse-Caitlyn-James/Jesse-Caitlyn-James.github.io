About Me

I am an aspiring software developer from Cairns, in the Far North Queensland. I love to solve problems and come to creative solutions to them. I'm always looking forward to ways to diversify my skills and learn something new.

I look to create new and innovative solutions to current problems. I want to use my skills to design and implement technology that will improve the lives of many. Most of all I want to be able to always reflect back on what i've done and what I did to get there.

I graduated high school in 2020 after pursuing biology and spending my free time managing AV and lighting in the theatre. In 2021 I completed a Certificate 3 in ICT at TAFE QLD where I explored my love for computer hardware and software development. After taking a gap year to work I am now in Griffith University studying a Diploma in ICT which I expect to graduate with at the end of this year, where I look forward to starting a Bachelors in ICT majoring in software development.

I'm pursuing a degree in ICT with a Major in Software Development. I'm looking to take primarily software development course with the goal in mind of getting a broad knowledge base and skillset across many coding languages and applications. I aim to be an valuable member of any team with an in depth knowledge of many languages and ways of solving problems in the future. My elective subjects will be quite similiar with many software development and application courses, with the inclusion of some circuit and hardware courses.

I aim to become a strong software developer. My goal is to be a valuable part of a team creating leading innovations and resources for the world. I'm motivated to create quality of life resources that increase the ease of use in everyday applications. I also wish to be part of the changing, innovative environments in the IT sector and hope to take advantage of new hardware and software applications, as well as learning all the new skills and methods they create.

My study path is another step in my journey to learn and adopt new skills and methods. I aim to take a variety of courses centered around software development, including software structures, games development, mobile development and more. I am also looking to study some base computer hardware and circuitry courses to better understand how the machines work and how I might better use them.
